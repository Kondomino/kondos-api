## Legend

- **Infos**: Basic property information (name, description, address, prices, etc.)
- **Conveniences**: Amenities and features (pool, gym, parking, etc.)
- **Images**: Property photos and visual media
- **Videos**: Video tours and multimedia content

## Notes

- ✅ **Yes**: Feature is successfully extracted
- ❌ **No**: Feature is not currently extracted or not available
- ⚠️ **Partial**: Feature is partially extracted (use when applicable)
